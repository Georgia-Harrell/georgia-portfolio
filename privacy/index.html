Privacy Policy

Relics & Returns  ·  Last updated: June 10, 2026

Relics & Returns ("the App") is an independently developed Android application built by Georgia Harrell. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and optional display name and phone number provided during sign-up or profile setup.
  • App content: Photos you take or upload for item scanning, item details you save to your collection, and marketplace listings you create.
  • Messages: In-app messages sent between buyers and sellers on the marketplace.
  • Usage data: Number of scans performed per day, subscription tier, and timestamps associated with app activity.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app features: item scanning, eBay price lookups, collection management, and the marketplace.
  • To authenticate your account and keep your data secure.
  • To enforce daily scan limits based on your subscription tier.
  • To send transactional email notifications (e.g., when a listing is claimed).

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage.
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI-powered item identification from photos.
  • eBay API — retrieval of publicly available sold listing prices.
  • Google Play Billing — in-app subscription purchases.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. Photos uploaded for scanning are used solely to identify the item and are not shared with third parties beyond the AI identification service. Reasonable security measures are in place, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Data Sharing

Your data is never sold. Information is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to operate the App. Marketplace listings and display names are visible to other users of the App by design.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. You can update your display name and phone number directly within the App under the Account tab.

Children's Privacy

Relics & Returns is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
